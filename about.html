About Me

Hi I'm Sven! I'm a Game Developer who's worked on many personal projects and experienced many different parts of game development. I've made multiple mobile and PC games in various genres, from 2D platformers to 3D adventure games.

I mainly love programming, but can also do some simple art, sound design, level design, modeling and video editing.

I have a little experience in everything as I made many personal projects that required different aspects of game design.

I also have experience making tools for Unity and Godot. Some of them are TileMap Procedural Generation for Godot written in C# and a library of UI Animations for Unity.
